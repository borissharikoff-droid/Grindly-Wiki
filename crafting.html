    ⚒️ Crafting

    Combine raw materials from chests, mob drops from the arena, and harvested crops from your farm to forge powerful gear at the workbench.

    Craft Flow

    Raw Materials → Intermediates → Gear

    How It Works

    • Raw materials drop from chests as bonus loot
    • Mob materials drop from arena mobs (Slime Gel, Goblin Tooth, etc.)
    • Farm crops are harvested from your farm plots
    • Refine raw materials into intermediates (Iron Bar, Fang Shard, etc.)
    • Combine intermediates + mob/farm materials to craft gear
    • Higher Crafter level unlocks better recipes and reduces craft time

    Level Milestones

    • Lvl 10: -10% craft time
    • Lvl 25: -20% craft time, 15% double output chance
    • Lvl 40: -30% craft time
    • Lvl 60: -45% craft time, 45% double output chance
    • Lvl 80: -60% craft time

    Each arena zone drops a unique material used to craft a gate item that unlocks the next zone.
