📄 What is Grindly?

Grindly is a Windows desktop productivity tracker that turns your daily computer usage into an RPG adventure. It monitors your active window usage, categorizes activities into skills, and rewards you with XP, levels, loot, and achievements.


Key Features:

  • 11 Skills — Developer, Designer, Gamer, Communicator, Researcher, Creator, Learner, Listener, Farmer, Warrior, Crafter
  • Loot System — Earn chests from focus sessions, open them for gear with stat bonuses
  • Arena Combat — Fight mobs and bosses across 6 dungeon zones for gold & materials
  • Crafting — Smelt ores, refine materials, and forge powerful equipment
  • Farming — Plant seeds, grow crops, and sell harvests on the marketplace
  • Social — Friends, chat, leaderboards via Supabase
  • Streaks & Achievements — Daily streaks, missions, and unlock badges

XP Formula

Each skill has 99 levels. XP required for level L:

xpForLevel(L) = floor(pow(L/99, 2.2) * 3,600,000)

Max XP (level 99) = 3,600,000. See Skills → XP Formula for full table.
