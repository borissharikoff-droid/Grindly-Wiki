Level Up 11 Skills Just By Using Your PC

Grindly tracks your active window and turns every minute into XP. Coding in VS Code? Developer XP. Browsing Figma? Designer XP. Playing games? Gamer XP. No manual input — it all happens automatically.

  • 8 passive skills that level from your daily PC usage
  • Warrior, Crafter & Farmer level from in-game activities
  • 99 levels per skill with exponential XP curve
  • XP multipliers from daily streaks (up to +30%)
  • Total level shown across all skills

Deep Analytics on Your Screen Time

AI-powered activity insights show exactly how you spend your time. Track focus quality, distraction share, context switches, and habits. See daily, weekly, monthly or all-time breakdowns with actionable recommendations.

  • Health Snapshot: tracked time, focus quality, distraction %
  • Context switch counter — fewer means deeper focus
  • Session stats: keystrokes, avg session length, streaks
  • Habits & Risks panel with personalised warnings
  • Today / 7d / 30d / All / Custom time ranges

Collect Loot, Equip Gear, Get Stronger

Focus sessions reward loot bags. Open them for equipment across 5 rarity tiers — Common to Mythic. Equip gear in 5 slots (Weapon, Head, Body, Legs, Ring) to boost ATK, HP, and IP. Open legendary bags for a chance at mythic drops with confetti and all.

  • 5 armor sets: Wooden, Copper, Shadow, Golden, Void
  • 5 equipment slots with visible stat boosts
  • Pity system guarantees rare+ drops after bad luck
  • Seeds, potions, materials & resources in inventory
  • Search & filter by category, rarity, type

Battle Bosses in the Arena

Enter 6 dungeon zones, each with unique mobs and a powerful boss. Fights are turn-based — your ATK and HP scale with equipped gear. Earn gold, rare materials, and Warrior XP. Craft boss-exclusive weapons from their drops.

  • 6 zones: Slime Cavern through Dragon Lair
  • Turn-based combat with ATK/s, HP regen, real stats
  • Character panel shows equipped gear & combat stats
  • Boss drops unlock exclusive crafting recipes
  • Gold rewards for buying on the marketplace

Craft Powerful Gear & Grow Your Farm

Smelt ores into bars, refine monster drops, and forge endgame weapons and armor. Plant seeds across 8 farm plots, harvest crops on timers, and use compost for bonus yields. Sell everything on the marketplace for gold.

  • 22 recipes from Compost to mythic-tier Void Blade
  • Multi-step crafting: raw drops → intermediates → gear
  • 9 seed types from Wheat (5 min) to Void Spore (8 hours)
  • Plant buffs that boost ATK, HP and XP in combat
  • Crafter & Farmer skills level as you work

Trade on the Player Marketplace

List your drops, buy upgrades from other players, and build the ultimate loadout. Per-unit pricing, category filters, and your own listings panel make trading fast. Add friends, chat in real-time, and compete on skill leaderboards.

  • Player-to-player trading with gold currency
  • Filter by Combat, XP, Drops, Cosmetic, Seeds
  • Partial buy — buy 1 or all from a stack listing
  • Friends list, real-time chat & online status
  • Per-skill leaderboards — compete with friends

XP Formula

Each skill has 99 levels. XP required for level L:

xpForLevel(L) = floor(pow(L/99, 2.2) * 3,600,000)

Max XP (level 99) = 3,600,000. See Skills → XP Formula for full table.
