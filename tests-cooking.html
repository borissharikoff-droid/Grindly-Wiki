    🔎 Notable Findings

    Issues and observations discovered during testing.

    Ambiguous Recipe Match: Apple Pie vs Apple Jam
    cook_apple_pie and cook_apple_jam share the same ingredient IDs (apples + wheat). Both matchRecipeFromIngredients and matchRecipeByIds always return Apple Jam first (earlier in array). This means a player cannot discover Apple Pie via the Cauldron — it will always match Apple Jam.
    "Steep & Strain" Maps to Mortar, Not Pot
    The step label "Steep & Strain" in cook_orchid_tea matches the mortar regex (/strain/) before the pot regex (/steep/) can match. This means Orchid Tea requires mortar (unlocked at level 25), which may be intentional for progression gating.
    Chef Double + Mastery Double Stack to 65%
    At max chef level (60+) the double output chance is 45%, and at 5-star mastery it adds 20% more. These stack additively to 65% total — high but still under 100%, which is correct.
