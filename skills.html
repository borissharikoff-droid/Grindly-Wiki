Levelling Curve

  • Each skill has 99 levels, designed to take roughly ~1,000 hours to max.
  • Core formula: xpForLevel(L) = floor(pow(L/99, 2.2) × 3,600,000)
  • Early levels (1–10): Triangular ramp — 120 × L(L+1) / 2
  • Levels 11–25: Smooth interpolation between the L10 and L25 breakpoints.
  • Levels 26+: Full exponential curve kicks in.
  • Maximum total skill level across all 11 skills: 11 × 99 = 1,089
