    🧪 Compost

    Compost is an organic fertiliser you apply to an empty farm plot before planting a seed. A composted plot boosts both the harvest yield and the Farmer XP you earn from that crop.

    Bonuses

    BonusValue
    Harvest Yield+35% (rounded up)
    Farmer XP on Harvest+5% (rounded up)

    How to Use

    • Each empty plot shows a Compost button if you have compost in your inventory.
    • Composting one plot costs 3 Compost.
    • A composted plot glows amber and displays the 🧪 icon. The bonus applies to whichever seed you plant next in that slot.
    • Compost is consumed on application — it cannot be removed once applied.

    Compost All

    At Farmer level 50 you unlock the Compost All button in the Farm header. It automatically composts every empty, un-composted plot in a single click (costs 3 Compost per plot).

    How to Obtain Compost

    SourceDetails
    Crafting Wheat ×5 + Herbs ×3 → Compost ×3  (Crafter Lvl 0, 60 XP, 6s)
    Harvest Drop 8% chance to receive 1 Compost as a bonus drop on any harvest.

    How Seeds Are Obtained

    Seeds are found inside Seed Zips that drop alongside loot chests. When you open a chest, there is a rarity-dependent chance that a Seed Zip is included. The Seed Zip contains a random seed whose rarity is weighted by the chest tier that produced it. Higher-tier chests yield rarer seeds more often.

    Seed Zip Chance by Chest Tier

    • Common chest — 40% Seed Zip chance
    • Rare chest — 55% Seed Zip chance
    • Epic chest — 70% Seed Zip chance
    • Legendary chest — 85% Seed Zip chance

    Bonus Harvest Zip

    Every harvest has a 15% chance to drop a bonus Seed Zip. Tier distribution: Common 60%, Rare 25%, Epic 12%, Legendary 3%.

    🗄 Seed Cabinet

    The Seed Cabinet is a dedicated storage for all your seeds. Once unlocked, any seeds in your inventory are automatically transferred into the cabinet. You can view and manage all stored seeds from the Farm page.

    Unlock Requirements

    The Seed Cabinet is locked by default. To unlock it you must meet all three conditions simultaneously:

    RequirementValue
    🌱 Farmer Skill Level20
    💧 Slime King Kills20
    🪙 Gold Cost3,000g (deducted on unlock)

    How It Works

    • Before unlocking, seeds sit in your Inventory backpack alongside regular items.
    • After unlocking, all seeds are moved to the cabinet automatically — including any future seeds you receive.
    • The cabinet displays each seed type with its quantity, rarity, and grow time.
    • You can plant seeds directly from the cabinet into your farm slots.
    • Seeds can also be sold on the Marketplace to other players.

    🥀 Crop Rot

    When you plant a seed, there is a chance the crop will rot before it finishes growing. If a crop rots it is destroyed and you receive a Wilted Plant instead. Rot is rolled at plant time — the game decides whether the crop will rot and at what point during growth (between 30% and 70% of grow time).

    Base Rot Chance by Seed Rarity

    Higher rarity seeds have a higher base chance of rotting.

    Seed RarityBase Rot Chance
    Common12%
    Rare17%
    Epic22%
    Legendary27%
    Mythic32%

    Reducing Rot

    • Each Farmhouse level reduces rot chance by 2% (flat). At max Farmhouse (LVL 10) that is −20% reduction.
    • Example: a Legendary seed normally has 27% rot. With Farmhouse LVL 5 (−10%) the effective chance drops to 17%.

    What Happens When a Crop Rots

    • The planted slot is cleared.
    • You receive 1 Wilted Plant in your inventory.
    • A 🥀 toast notification appears: “Crop rotted!”
    • Rot is checked every 5 seconds while the app is running.

    🏠 Farmhouse

    The Farmhouse is a permanent building that provides powerful passive bonuses to all your farming activities. It unlocks at Farmer Level 45 and can be upgraded from Level 1 to 10. Each upgrade requires gold, materials, and a construction time.

    Construction

    • When you start a build, gold and materials are deducted immediately.
    • A progress bar appears showing construction progress and remaining time.
    • Construction continues even while you are on other pages — but the app must be running.
    • Once the timer completes, the upgrade is applied automatically and bonuses take effect.
    • You cannot start a new upgrade while one is already in progress.
