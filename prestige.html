    ✨ Prestige System

    Reached level 99 in a skill? Prestige to reset your level, earn a permanent XP bonus, and unlock exclusive cosmetic rewards. Up to 5 tiers per skill.

    💡 Overview

    Prestige is an endgame progression system that lets you reset a maxed skill back to level 0 in exchange for permanent bonuses and cosmetics. It is available for any skill once it reaches level 99.

    Each skill can be prestiged independently up to 5 times (5 tiers). Every prestige tier grants a stacking +5% XP bonus for that skill, making subsequent level-ups faster. Prestige also contributes bonus levels to your total skill level on the leaderboard.

    🔄 How It Works

    1
    Reach Level 99
    Max out any skill by earning 3,600,000 XP (approximately 1,000 hours of tracked activity). The prestige button appears once you hit level 99.
    2
    Confirm Prestige
    Click the prestige button on the skill panel. Your skill XP resets to 0 and your level drops back to 0. This cannot be undone.
    3
    Earn Rewards
    Gain a permanent +5% XP multiplier for that skill, a cosmetic upgrade (border, badge, frame, avatar, or title), and +99 bonus levels on the leaderboard.
    4
    Repeat
    Level the skill back to 99 and prestige again, up to 5 times total. Each tier stacks the XP bonus and unlocks a new cosmetic reward.

    📈 XP Bonus per Tier

    Each prestige tier adds a flat +5% XP multiplier for the prestiged skill. This stacks linearly:

    xpMultiplier = 1 + (prestigeCount × 0.05)
    Tier 1 = ×1.05 (+5%) · Tier 2 = ×1.10 (+10%) · Tier 3 = ×1.15 (+15%) · Tier 4 = ×1.20 (+20%) · Tier 5 = ×1.25 (+25%)

    This multiplier stacks multiplicatively with other XP sources (Grindly skill bonus, equipped gear). For example, at Prestige Tier 5 (×1.25) with max Grindly (×1.43): 1.25 × 1.43 = ×1.7875 — nearly 79% bonus XP.

    🏅 Leaderboard Impact

    Each prestige completion adds 99 bonus levels to your total skill level for leaderboard ranking purposes. This means a prestiged player will always rank higher than a non-prestiged player with the same current levels.

    totalLevel = sum(currentLevels) + sum(prestigeCount × 99) per skill
    The maximum possible total with all 13 skills at level 99 and prestige tier 5 each: 13 × 99 + 13 × 5 × 99 = 1,287 + 6,435 = 7,722

    📚 Strategy Tips

    • Prestige your primary skill first. The skill you use most will benefit the most from the XP bonus, since you will be earning XP for it constantly.
    • Stack with Grindly bonuses. The prestige XP multiplier stacks multiplicatively with the Grindly skill XP bonus (up to +43% at Grindly level 99). Combined, this dramatically cuts leveling time.
    • Consider leaderboard impact. Each prestige adds 99 effective levels. If you are competing on the leaderboard, prestiging multiple skills will push your total far beyond the base cap of 1,287.
    • Plan for the time investment. Each prestige cycle requires leveling from 0 to 99 again (~1,000 hours base). With the XP bonus from prior prestiges, each subsequent cycle is faster: Tier 1 cuts it to ~952h, Tier 4 cuts it to ~833h.
    • In-game skills (Warrior, Crafter, Farmer, Chef) prestige too. While these level through gameplay rather than tracking, the same prestige rules apply. Warrior prestige is particularly valuable for the leaderboard boost.
    • Cosmetic rewards are permanent. Even while re-leveling after a prestige, your earned borders, badges, frames, and titles remain visible on your profile.

    ❓ FAQ

    Do I lose my items or gold when I prestige?

    No. Prestige only resets the skill's XP and level. Your inventory, gold, equipment, farm, and all other progress remain untouched.

    Can I prestige all skills?

    Yes. Every skill in the game can be prestiged independently up to 5 times, including in-game skills like Warrior, Crafter, Farmer, and Chef.

    Does the XP bonus apply retroactively?

    The prestige XP bonus applies to all future XP earned for that skill. It does not retroactively change existing progress on other skills.

    What happens after Tier 5?

    Tier 5 (Void) is the maximum. After reaching it, the skill can still be leveled to 99 but cannot be prestiged further. You keep the full +25% XP bonus and all cosmetic rewards permanently.
