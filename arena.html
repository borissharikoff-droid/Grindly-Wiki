Arena & Bosses

Battle through 6 zones, defeat mobs for XP & gold, and challenge bosses for rare loot bags.

6Zones
18Mobs
6Bosses

⚔️ Combat Zones

Each zone contains three mob tiers and a boss encounter. Defeat the boss to unlock the next zone. Mobs drop gold, Warrior XP, and crafting materials. Bosses award large XP bonuses and loot bags.
