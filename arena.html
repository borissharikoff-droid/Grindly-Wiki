    Arena & Bosses

    Battle through 6 zones, defeat mobs for XP & gold, and challenge bosses for a chance at loot bags. Boss chest drops are not guaranteed.

    6Zones
    18Mobs

    ⚔️ Combat Zones

    Each zone contains three mob tiers and a boss encounter. Defeat the boss to unlock the next zone.

    Combat Mechanics

    Battles are tick-based (0.5s ticks) with dynamic damage variance. Each tick, both sides deal damage simultaneously.

    MechanicFormulaNotes
    Player DPSmax(ATK × 15%, ATK − Enemy DEF)You always deal at least 15% of your ATK, even against high DEF
    Enemy DPSmax(ATK × 15%, ATK − Player DEF − Player Regen)Your DEF and HP Regen both reduce incoming damage
    Damage Variance±20% (player) / ±20-35% (bosses)Each tick rolls random damage within the spread range
    Base Stats5 ATK, 100 HP, 0 DEF, 0 RegenYour starting stats before any gear or warrior bonuses
    VictoryEnemy HP reaches 0 before yoursBoss fights have a 10 min timeout (= loss)

    Boss Chest Drop Mechanics

    Boss chest drops are not guaranteed. When you defeat a boss, the following roll determines your chest reward:

    RollChanceResult
    Listed chest tier55%You receive the chest shown in the boss reward (e.g. Rare Bag)
    One tier lower30%You receive a chest one rarity below (e.g. Rare → Common). Common stays Common.
    No chest15%No chest at all — you only keep gold and materials earned from mob kills

    Even when a chest drops, equipment is not guaranteed. Each chest tier has a separate equipment chance:

    ChestEquipment ChanceGold RangeOn Fail
    Common Bag50%15–30gGold + 1 bonus material roll
    Rare Bag60%30–75gGold + 2 bonus material rolls
    Epic Bag70%75–150gGold + 2 bonus material rolls
    Legendary Bag80%150–300gGold + 3 bonus material rolls

    Zone 1 Strategy

    • Entry-level zone — no gear or warrior level required, but costs 3 Wheat per run (farm Wheat Seeds on your farm)
    • Farm Slime Gel from mobs (30-50% drop chance) and boss (3 guaranteed) to craft Slime Shield
    • Slime Shield is a gate item required to enter Zone 3 (Wild Forest) — you must own it but it is not consumed
    • Equip the Wooden Set from Common Bags to reach 11 ATK / 115 HP / 1 Regen
