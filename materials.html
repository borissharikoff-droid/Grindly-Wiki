🔨 Materials

Materials are the building blocks of the crafting system. They come from chests, the smelter, arena mobs, and boss kills.

Raw Crafting Materials (from Chests)

MaterialRaritySource
Iron Ore Common Common to Epic chests
Monster Fang Common Common to Epic chests
Magic Essence Rare Rare+ chests
Ancient Scale Rare Epic+ chests
Void Crystal Epic Epic+ chests

Intermediate Materials (Smelted)

Raw materials are refined at the workbench into intermediate components used in gear recipes.

OutputRecipe
Iron Bar Iron Ore ×5
Fang Shard Monster Fang ×4
Essence Vial Magic Essence ×3
Ancient Dust Ancient Scale ×2
Void Fragment Void Crystal ×2

Arena Mob Materials

Defeating mobs in the arena has a chance to drop zone-specific crafting materials.

MaterialZoneDrop Rate
Slime Gel Slime Cavern 30-50%
Goblin Tooth Goblin Outpost 30-50%
Wolf Fang Wild Forest 30-50%
Orc Shard Orc Stronghold 30-50%
Troll Hide Troll Bridge 30-50%
Dragon Scale Dragon Lair 30-50%

Boss-Exclusive Materials
