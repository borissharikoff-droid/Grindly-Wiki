Raw materials drop as bonus loot from chests. Higher-tier chests give rarer materials.

MaterialRaritySourceUsed For
🪨 Iron Ore Common Common to Epic chests Iron Bar
🦷 Monster Fang Common Common to Epic chests Fang Shard
💧 Magic Essence Rare Rare+ chests Essence Vial
🐉 Ancient Scale Rare Epic+ chests Ancient Dust
🔮 Void Crystal Epic Epic+ chests Void Fragment

Raw materials are refined at the workbench into intermediate components used in gear recipes.

OutputRarityRecipeCrafter LvlXP
⬛ Iron Bar Common Iron Ore ×5 Lvl 0 45 XP
🦴 Fang Shard Common Monster Fang ×4 Lvl 0 55 XP
🧪 Essence Vial Rare Magic Essence ×3 Lvl 15 20 XP
💨 Ancient Dust Rare Ancient Scale ×2 Lvl 30 35 XP
💠 Void Fragment Epic Void Crystal ×2 Lvl 55 60 XP

Defeating mobs in the arena has a chance to drop zone-specific crafting materials.

MaterialRarityZoneDrop RateUsed For
🫧 Slime Gel Common Slime Cavern 30-50% Slime Shield
🦷 Goblin Tooth Common Goblin Outpost 30-50% Goblin Blade
🐺 Wolf Fang Rare Wild Forest 30-50% Wolf Fang Pendant
🪨 Orc Shard Rare Orc Stronghold 30-50% Orc Plate
🧌 Troll Hide Epic Troll Bridge 30-50% Troll Cloak
🐉 Dragon Scale Legendary Dragon Lair 30-50% Dragon Crown

These rare materials only drop from defeating zone bosses. Each boss drops exactly one on kill. Used for endgame crafting recipes.

MaterialRarityBossDrop QtyUsed For
🔱 Warlord Sigil Epic Orc Warlord ×1 Warlord Gauntlets
💜 Troll Heart Legendary Troll Overlord ×1 Troll Aegis
