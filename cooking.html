    🍳 Cooking

    Prepare ingredients and cook dishes that grant temporary arena buffs. Level up your Chef skill to unlock better recipes and reduce burn chance.

    Cook Flow

    Gather Ingredients → Select Recipe → Cook Dish → Eat for Buff

    How It Works

    • Combine farm crops and mob materials to cook dishes
    • 16 recipes across all rarity tiers (Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythic) plus a Mystery Stew
    • Your Chef skill levels up as you cook — higher level unlocks better recipes
    • Cooked food provides temporary arena buffs (Heal HP, ATK, DEF, Regen boosts lasting a set duration)
    • Instruments improve cook speed, quality, and reduce burn chance
    • Mastery — cook a dish repeatedly to earn stars and bonus multipliers

    Buff System

    • Each dish grants a temporary buff that lasts for a set duration (in seconds)
    • Buffs can boost ATK, HP, DEF, or HP Regen (some dishes boost multiple stats)
    • Only one food buff can be active at a time — eating a new dish replaces the current buff
    • Perfect quality dishes grant 150% of the listed buff value

    🍳 Cooking Instruments

    You have 6 instrument slots, each with 5 upgrade tiers. Better instruments improve cook speed, dish quality, and reduce burn chance. Some instruments must be unlocked by reaching a Chef level and paying gold.

    Tier Bonuses

    Each upgrade tier provides cumulative bonuses across all instruments:

    • Speed Bonus — reduces cook time
    • Quality Bonus — increases chance of Perfect quality dishes
    • Burn Reduction — reduces chance of burning dishes

    Upgrade Notes

    • Each instrument upgrades independently through its 5 tiers
    • Higher tiers require gold and materials to unlock
    • Bonuses from all instruments stack together
    • Unlock all instruments and upgrade them to Mythril for maximum cooking efficiency

    ⭐ Recipe Mastery

    Each recipe has its own mastery level (1–5 stars) that increases as you cook the same dish repeatedly. Higher mastery provides permanent bonuses for that recipe.

    Star Bonuses

    Each star earned on a recipe grants cumulative bonuses:

    • Buff Multiplier — increases the strength of the dish's buff
    • Ingredient Save Chance — chance to not consume ingredients when cooking
    • Double Output Chance — chance to produce two dishes from one cook
    • XP Multiplier — increases Chef XP earned from cooking this dish

    Tips

    • Focus on mastering common recipes first — they are cheap to cook and the ingredient save chance helps with rarer dishes later
    • 5-star mastery on a recipe makes it significantly more efficient to produce
    • Mastery is tracked per recipe, not globally — each dish has its own progress

    🔥 Burn Chance

    Every time you cook a dish, there is a chance it will burn. Burn chance depends on the recipe difficulty, your Chef level, and your instrument quality.

    What Affects Burn Chance

    • Each recipe has a base burn chance (higher rarity = higher base burn chance)
    • Higher Chef level reduces burn chance
    • Better instruments reduce burn chance
    • Burn chance can never go below 0%

    Quality Tiers

    QualityBuff StrengthChef XP
    Burned No buff (0%) Reduced XP
    Normal 100% buff Full XP
    Perfect 150% buff Full XP + bonus

    Tips

    • Start with common recipes to level your Chef skill safely
    • Upgrade your instruments before attempting epic or legendary recipes
    • Even burned food gives some Chef XP, so cooking is never a total waste
