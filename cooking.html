    🍳 Cooking

    Prepare ingredients and cook dishes that grant temporary arena buffs. Level up your Chef skill to unlock better recipes and reduce burn chance.

    🚧 Work in Progress

    The Cooking system is currently in development for v3.5. All values shown below are planned and subject to change before release.

    Cook Flow

    Raw Ingredients → Prep → Cook Dish

    How It Works

    • Two-step process: First prep raw ingredients, then cook the final dish
    • 16 recipes are planned across all rarity tiers
    • Your Chef skill levels up as you cook — higher level unlocks better recipes
    • Cooked food provides temporary arena buffs (ATK, HP, HP Regen boosts lasting a set number of fights)
    • Quality mechanic: higher Chef level and better kitchen instruments reduce burn chance, yielding better quality food

    Buff System

    • Each dish grants a temporary buff that lasts for a certain number of arena fights
    • Buffs can boost ATK, HP, or HP Regen (some dishes boost multiple stats)
    • Only one food buff can be active at a time — eating a new dish replaces the current buff
    • Perfect quality dishes grant 150% of the listed buff value

    🍳 Kitchen Instruments

    Your kitchen determines how fast you cook and your chance of producing higher quality food. You start with a Basic Campfire and can upgrade by spending gold once you reach the required Chef level.

    InstrumentChef LvlGold CostCook TimeQuality Bonus
    Basic Campfire Lvl 1 Free Base —
    Stone Oven Lvl 10 1,000g -15% +10% Perfect chance
    Iron Stove Lvl 25 5,000g -30% +20% Perfect chance
    Master Kitchen Lvl 50 15,000g -50% +35% Perfect chance

    Upgrade Notes

    • Each instrument replaces the previous one — you only have one active kitchen at a time
    • Better instruments reduce cook time and improve your chance of getting Perfect quality dishes
    • Cook time reduction stacks with Chef Perk time reductions

    Level Milestones

    • Lvl 5: -10% cook time
    • Lvl 15: -15% cook time, 10% double output chance
    • Lvl 30: -25% cook time, 20% double output chance
    • Lvl 50: -35% cook time, Instant Prep for common recipes
    • Lvl 70: -50% cook time, 35% double output chance

    How Chef XP Works

    • You earn Chef XP every time you successfully cook a dish (even burned dishes give some XP)
    • Higher rarity recipes grant more XP per cook
    • Perk bonuses are applied automatically once you reach the required level
    • Cook time reductions from perks stack with kitchen instrument bonuses

    🔥 Burn Chance

    Every time you cook a dish, there is a chance it will burn. Burn chance depends on the recipe difficulty, your Chef level, and your kitchen instrument.

    What Affects Burn Chance

    • Each recipe has a base burn chance (higher rarity = higher base burn chance)
    • Higher Chef level reduces burn chance
    • Better kitchen instruments reduce burn chance
    • Burn chance can never go below 0%

    Quality Tiers

    QualityBuff StrengthChef XP
    Burned No buff (0%) Reduced XP
    Normal 100% buff Full XP
    Perfect 150% buff Full XP + bonus

    Tips

    • Start with common recipes to level your Chef skill safely
    • Upgrade your kitchen before attempting epic or legendary recipes
    • Even burned food gives some Chef XP, so cooking is never a total waste
