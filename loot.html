🎁 Loot & Chests

Chests drop at the end of productive sessions. Each chest contains gear, crafting materials, gold, and possibly a Seed Zip.

NameRarityBase Drop RateBonus MaterialsGold Range
📦 Common Bag Common 70% Iron Ore (1-2) + Monster Fang (1-2) 15-30g
🎁 Rare Bag Rare 22% Iron Ore (1-3) + Monster Fang (1-3) + Magic Essence (1-2) 30-75g
🪙 Epic Bag Epic 7% Iron Ore (2-4) + Monster Fang (2-4) + Magic Essence (1-3) + Ancient Scale (1-2) + Void Crystal (1) 75-150g
💎 Legendary Bag Legendary 1% Magic Essence (2-4) + Ancient Scale (2-3) + Void Crystal (1-2) 150-300g

Guaranteed Upgrades

The pity system ensures you receive higher-rarity chests after a streak of lower-tier drops:

  • 15 rolls without a Rare → guaranteed Rare chest
  • 40 rolls without an Epic → guaranteed Epic chest
  • 80 rolls without a Legendary → guaranteed Legendary chest

Seed Zip by Chest Tier

Each chest tier has a different probability of including a Seed Zip:

  • Common chest — 40% Seed Zip chance
  • Rare chest — 55% Seed Zip chance
  • Epic chest — 70% Seed Zip chance
  • Legendary chest — 85% Seed Zip chance

Activity-Based Boosts

Certain activities modify your chest drop rates:

  • daily_activity: Active daily usage provides a boost to chest drop rates
  • goal_complete: Completing daily goals gives a slight boost
  • skill_grind (coding): Extended coding sessions provide a slight boost
