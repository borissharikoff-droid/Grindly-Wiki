Each bag also rolls bonus crafting materials. Higher-tier bags roll more times and from rarer material pools.

BagRollsMaterial PoolQty Range
Common 1 Iron Ore · Monster Fang 1–2 per roll
Rare 2 Iron Ore · Monster Fang · Magic Essence 1–3 per roll
Epic 2 Iron Ore · Monster Fang · Magic Essence · Ancient Scale · Void Crystal 1–4 per roll
Legendary 3 Magic Essence · Ancient Scale · Void Crystal 1–4 per roll

How Bonus Rolls Work

  • Each roll picks one material from the pool using weighted random selection
  • The quantity per roll is random within the range shown
  • Multiple rolls can pick the same material — quantities stack
  • These materials are in addition to the guaranteed gear piece

Guaranteed Upgrades

The pity system ensures you receive higher-rarity bags after a streak of lower-tier drops:

ConditionGuarantee
15 rolls without a Rare bagNext bag is guaranteed Rare
40 rolls without an Epic bagNext bag is guaranteed Epic
80 rolls without a Legendary bagNext bag is guaranteed Legendary

Pity Counter Rules

  • Rare pity counter resets whenever you receive any bag that is Rare or above
  • Epic pity counter resets only on Epic or Legendary bags
  • Legendary pity counter resets only on Legendary bags
  • Pity triggers override the normal roll — Legendary check happens first, then Epic, then Rare
